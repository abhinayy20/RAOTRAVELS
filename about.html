Who We Are

RAO Travels was founded in 2010 with a single dream — to make luxury travel accessible and unforgettable for every Indian family. What began as a small agency in Basti, Uttar Pradesh, has grown into a trusted name across the country, having served over 25,000 happy clients across 50+ destinations worldwide.

Every itinerary we craft is a labor of love. We don't just book tickets — we curate experiences that become lifelong memories.

Our Mission

To deliver world-class travel experiences that are seamlessly planned, deeply personal, and priced with honesty — making every rupee feel priceless.

Our Vision

To become India's most loved premium travel brand — where every traveler feels understood, valued, and inspired to see more of the world.
