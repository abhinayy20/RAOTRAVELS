Duration 5 Days / 4 Nights

Group Size Max 12 People

Tour Overview

Experience the majestic Himalayas with our Mystic Himachal Retreat. This carefully curated 5-day journey takes you through the colonial charm of Shimla and the breathtaking valleys of Manali. Perfect for nature lovers and thrill-seekers alike, this tour promises an unforgettable blend of serene landscapes and rich cultural heritage.

Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Shimla & Local Sightseeing

Arrive in Shimla, check-in to your premium hotel. In the evening, explore the famous Mall Road, The Ridge, and Christ Church. Enjoy a welcome dinner.

Day 2

Kufri Excursion

After breakfast, head to Kufri. Enjoy panoramic views, visit the Himalayan Nature Park, and experience horse riding. Return to Shimla for the night.

Day 3

Transfer to Manali via Kullu

Scenic drive from Shimla to Manali. En route, visit the Kullu Valley, Pandoh Dam, and local shawl factories. Check-in at your riverside resort in Manali.

Day 4

Solang Valley Adventure

Full day excursion to Solang Valley. Indulge in adventure sports like paragliding, zorbing, and skiing (subject to weather). Evening at leisure.

Day 5

Departure

After breakfast, check-out and transfer to the airport/railway station for your onward journey with beautiful memories.
